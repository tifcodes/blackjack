how to play blackjack

  • The goal of the game is to beat the dealer and getting the total close to 21
  • The game starts with 2 cards on the page
  • If your cards is equal to 21, you win
  • If your cards add to less than 21 but higher than dealer, you win
  • If your cards add to more than 21, you lose
  • Value of the Cards: A is 11; J, Q and K is 10 and the numbers are worth itself
  • If you want to add more cards, click the "hit" button
  • If you don't want any more cards, click the "stand" button
  • Click "new game" to start the game again
